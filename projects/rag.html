RAG Chatbot with IBM Watsonx & LangChain

This Retrieval-Augmented Generation chatbot combines document embeddings with large language models to answer questions based on uploaded PDFs. Built with LangChain and IBM Watsonx, it enables dynamic, context-aware interactions through a Gradio-based frontend.

Technologies Used: Python, IBM Watsonx, LangChain, Gradio, Chroma
